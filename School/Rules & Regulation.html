Rules & Regulation

School Rules And Regulations

  • Working hours:8:00 a.m to 3:30 p.m.(2:00-9:30)Office hours;8:30 a.m.-12:00 noon.

Students'Behavior And Conduct
  • It is the duty of every stuent to know the school rules and observe them at all times.
  • Self discipline is necessary to achieve self direction.Thus the school is help to strength the students determonation for integral growth.They are guidelines to self development rather than restrictions to it.
  • the school start at 8:00 a.m. and all the students are required to be in the school by this time.The school gates will be closed at 8:00 a.m.
  • Every stuent is expected to be present for the daily assembly
    No students will remain in the classrooms when the bell rings.
  • Students who absent themselves from the school will be required to bring a note in their communication book to the school office from the parents or guardians explaining the reasons for their absence.The Head Mistress may then permit the student to enter the class.if there is any doubt concerning the one who has written the note for the student who had been absent will be sent home till his/her parent or guardians comes to school
  • No student will be permitted to leave the school premises at any time of the school hours without a written request from the parents or guardians addressed to Head Mistress,Any violation of this Regulation may result in the immediate suspension or dismissal of the student
  • parents or guardians of the students who are absent must notify the school by means of note in the communication book on the first day return to the school
  • Absence without proper information will not be tolerate
      • Uniform And Books
      • All students are expected to come to school with neat and clean uniform every day.All students must wear uniform approved by the school.Neatness and cleanliness of dress and person is a must.The school reserves the right to send back home those who do not conform to the prescribe uniform and apperance code.
      • No high hell and open shoes should be worn with uniform.Lip sticks,painted nails, jewerly, watches, bleached hair etc.
        are not permitted in the school.
      • Boys should keep their hair short and no bleaching is permitted
      • Students are responsible for the loss of personal property.
      • Text books and exercise books should be brought to school daily according to the time table.
      • Students are absolutely forbidden to bring any material other than those required for their studies to the school.
      • Any student found bringing knives and other instruments to school will be immidiately dismissed from the school.
      • Communication book should be brought to school daily.
      • parents should check their children's exercise books regularly to see what have done in the class and to assist them in their homework.
      Accidents

      • In case of small cuts and wounds the school will provide the first aid.If something more serious happens the parents will be called for immiediately and they are expected to come to the school to take their children.

      Exams
      • All student should be present on all examination days.Those remaining absent will not be permitted to write the exams later on.
      • Those students who absent themselves for reasons of health should notify the school office immediately and a medical certiicate should be submitted.
      • Malpractices like copying and cheating will not be tolerated...
        Those caught copying will not be permitted to answer the exams.
      Promotions

      • Promotion to the next class will depend on the whole year's performance.
        An average of 65% marks is required for promotion to the next class.

      Library Rules

      • The Library is Open to all the members of the school on all working days.Puplis should visit the Library frequently and do intellegent reading and refer books without neglecting good reading.

      1. Strict silence should be observed in the Library
      2. No one shall tame any printed book into the Library without the permission of the librarian
      3. Books should not be marked of defaces.Pages should not be folded and used as book marks.puplis should examine each book before borrowing it and report to the librarian any damage found there in. Should they fail to do so they will be held responsible for any damage detected after their use of the book.
      4. Reference books and encyclopedias will not be issued but are available for reference within the Library.
      5. Dates and label should not be tampered with
      6. The librarian might recall a book at any time even basfore their expiry of the normal period loan
      7. NO pupil is allowed to lend books to any one in or outside the school. library books will not lend either to pupils or staff during the vacations.
      8. If the pages of the books are torn or dirty the students will have to pay for the books
      Class Room Rules And Regulations

      • Students are required to follow the following rules and Regulations in the class room.

      1. Stuents should come to school on time with clean uniform.The nails should be cut regularly.Black closed shoes should be worn to school.Gils should comb their hair and plait them in five lines and no beads and hair decorations should be allowed in the school.
      2. Students are expected to stand and greet their teacher when he/she enters the class.
      3. Should pay attention in the class and not be engaged in other activities while the lesson is in progress.
      4. No students should leave the class room without the permission of the teachers.
      5. Should complete the home-work or class-work on time and give to the teachers for correction.
      6. Respect teachers and classmates
      7. Fighting in the class room or outside, shouting and insulting is strictly for bidden.
      8. No stuents are allowed to stay in class room during the breaks
      Admission And Withdrawal
      1. New students will be given introduced personally by the parent/guardian who will be responsible to the principal for their regularity and fees.
      2. The students will be given an entrance exam and only those who qualify will be admitted into the school.
      3. Students coming from other schools are required to produce transfer certificate from the school last attended.
      4. Transfer certificate will be give only to those students who have fully settled their dues and produced a written application from the parent/guardian.
      5. The application should be made two weeks before the withdrawal or before 25th june E.C.
      6. Those leaving the school during the year will be required to pay fees for the full year. This penalty is charged as the delay in talking the transfer certificate deprives another child from admission into Auxilium.

      7. Those who are already attending school should come at the given time for the registration. Those who delay will have to pay the penalty. It will be decided by the school.
      Dismissal

      • The school has the right to dismiss students who misbehave

      1. Insulting and fighting
      2. Using bad language
      3. Robbing in the school and outside.
      4. Destroying school property
      5. Repeated complaints from the teachers
      6. And other serious offenses not listed here.
      7. Parents who contradict the school policy will be asked to remove their children rom the school
