Services

This Broker company is a free online Market website in Ethiopia. Buy, sell and rent real estate properties in Ethiopia. Broker company Real Estate lists various homes and properties for sale and rent in Ethiopia. Registered users can create an account with us in a few minutes and post real estate ads free of charge. Our online tools allow you to manage your ads fully, including creating ads, modifying ads, uploading multiple pictures, activating and deactivating ads, etc. Through our Private Message Board (PMB), you can communicate with potential buyers or renters for your property. You can arrange for site visits, negotiate prices, etc. When listing a property, whether it is for sale or rent, we strongly recommend that you include images of your properties in your ad (such as exterior and interior pictures of your house). This will allow potential buyers to see what you are trying to sell or rent, and get you the right buyers quickly. Prospective buyers or renters of properties can browse our listings at any time. If you are interested on any particular property, you can contact the seller directly by sending email or through our PMB. You can arrange for site tour or negotiate price.
